## 🎨 Color Usage Guide

### Primary Actions:
- Use: Teal (#14b8a6)
- Example: Book Now buttons, Links

### Secondary Actions:
- Use: Cyan (#0b7ce6)
- Example: Learn More, Navigation

### Backgrounds:
- Light: Cyan-50, Teal-50
- Dark: Teal-600 to Blue-600 gradients

### Text:
- Headings: Gray-900
- Body: Gray-700, Gray-600
- Subtle: Gray-500, Gray-400

### Accents:
- Trust badges: Teal-600
- Ratings: Gold (#eab308)
- Success: Green-500
- Warning: Amber-500
